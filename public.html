North Georgia Astronomical Observatory

The NGAO is a small, student run observatory located about 4 miles away from UNG-Dahlonega's campus. We have a staff of six undergraduate students working under the direction of Dr. Feiden who operate our 4 telescopes:

  • 16-in Cassegrain reflector (Boller & Chivens)
  • 12-in Cassegrain reflector (Meade)
  • 8-in Dobsonian reflector (Orion)
  • 4-in Newtonian reflector (Celestron)
NGAO is open every clear night Monday through Saturday, with a dedicated public viewing session on Friday evening. Check out our Facebook page or our Twitter feed for additional information.
